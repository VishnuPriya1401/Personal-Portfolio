Movie/ Drama App

Tool: Figma

Goal & Target audience

This Drama App allows users to view, download their favorite drama and movies of language Korean, Thai, Mandarin, Taiwanese. Users can also filter their search results according to their preference of the language and genre.

This App is designed with the targeted audience who loves watching Korean, Mandarin, Thai dramas and movies. This App id designed in such a way that it allows users for easy access through phone or tablet
