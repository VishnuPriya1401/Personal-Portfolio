Restaurant App

Tool: Figma

Goal of the Project

The main idea of this app is to create a user-friendly restaurant app. This app provides the users a quick and easy way of ordering food and book tables in advance. In this busy society, most people tend to skip food or are too lazy to search and order their favorite ones, this app is designed in a way that helps the users to create their own menu with favorite option, it also provides an easy repeat-menu option with name tag to enjoy their favorite dishes ordered easy and quick.

Target audience

This App is designed to target people who are workaholic, prefer to order food to save time and those who like to book tables in advance.
